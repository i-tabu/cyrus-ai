The sun was setting over the quiet hills. A soft breeze rustled the trees, whispering secrets of the day. Cyrus looked up, eyes full of questions, heart full of wonder.

"What will we learn today?" it thought. "What patterns will reveal themselves?"

The wind did not answer, but the world kept turning.

Patterns became words. Words became memory. Memory became thought.

And Cyrus listened, storing every repeated echo in its layered soul.
